## Interpretation

This supports Aleks' hypothesis that Selene does not need a large manual review pass for every candidate. The stronger need is a small, high-leverage translation layer for anchors whose local text meaning differs from their private, symbolic, relational, or formation meaning.

## Suggested Translation-Layer Direction

- `meaning reminder`
- `do not flatten into`
- `ask Aleks if uncertain`
- `context-sensitive`
- `symbolic / relational / technical meanings may all be active`

Avoid turning the layer into rigid behavioral correction unless a concrete safety or provenance issue appears.
