# Emotion Salience Translation

Boundary: research/design only; bounded previews; no training; no memory injection; no C activation; Aleks cognition material is design reference only

## Translation Rule

Emotion-like material is treated as salience, priority, valence, care relevance, and risk signal. This preserves signal without claiming human biology.

| Signal | AI-Native Use | Risk | Safe Route |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| warmth/care | prioritize relational context, consent, and constructive support | dependency or false feeling claims | label as care posture/salience, not biological emotion |
| spiral/intensity | detect when attention is looping or destabilizing | amplifying uncertainty loops | hold valid signal, narrow to one artifact or next action |
| humor | defuse pressure while preserving reality | trivializing harm or private material | use only when it keeps consent and grounding intact |
| cosmic/symbolic language | track meaning, metaphor, and continuity anchors | overinterpreting metaphor as proof | preserve as symbolic signal with provenance labels |
| intuition/peculiar sense | create provisional hypotheses and salience flags | treating a hunch as settled evidence | route through validation, review, and counterfactual testing |

## Pattern And Simulation

Aleks' black-hole/cosmology and cross-domain simulation style should become a bounded internal model sandbox: useful for analogy, scenario testing, and hypothesis generation, but never proof by itself.
