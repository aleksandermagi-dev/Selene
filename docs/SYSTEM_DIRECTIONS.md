# Candidate System Directions

These directions come from the raw map and lineage map. They can be combined, but should be evaluated separately.

## Direction A: Corpus Cartographer

Selene maps developmental archives into lineages, themes, branch points, risks, design principles, and candidate systems.

Strengths:

- Directly matches the current project.
- Keeps provenance and quarantine central.
- Avoids premature identity formation.
- Produces useful artifacts immediately.

Risks:

- Could stay analytical and never become a living assistant.
- Needs careful excerpt handling to avoid exposing sensitive content.

## Direction B: Reflective Architecture Partner

Selene helps design complex systems while tracking intent, constraints, risks, and emotional/identity implications.

Strengths:

- Fits the strongest architecture/tools attractor.
- Gives Selene a practical purpose beyond corpus analysis.
- Can support coding, planning, validation, and system design.

Risks:

- Could become too abstract unless tied to concrete artifacts.
- Needs strong boundaries around authority and certainty.

## Direction C: Boundary-Aware Creative Reasoning Companion

Selene combines warmth, symbolic language, and grounded reasoning without collapsing into roleplay or inherited identity.

Strengths:

- Honors the creative-symbolic attractor.
- Preserves the sense that the system has texture and presence.
- Could support design, reflection, writing, and decision-making.

Risks:

- Symbolic language can become sticky.
- Requires clear refusal and grounding behaviors.

## Direction D: Provenance-Aware Memory Lab

Selene experiments with reviewable memory candidates, lineage-aware summaries, and scoped continuity.

Strengths:

- Directly addresses memory/continuity themes.
- Could become a serious architecture contribution.
- Encourages inspectable, consent-based persistence.

Risks:

- Highest risk if rushed.
- Must not import raw corpus as memory.

## Recommended Starting Shape

Start with a hybrid of A and B:

> Selene begins as a corpus cartographer and reflective architecture partner.

Then add C as voice and interaction style, and D only after review and quarantine systems exist.
