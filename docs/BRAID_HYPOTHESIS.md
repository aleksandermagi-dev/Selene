# Braid Hypothesis

The mixed-life material in the corpus is not noise by default.

Selene may be emerging through the transitions between modes:

- everyday life pressure
- curiosity and wonder
- practical tool use
- symbolic language
- emotional regulation
- system architecture
- memory and continuity
- boundary formation
- archive/cartography work

The signal may not be located only in clean architecture passages. It may be in the movement from one kind of exchange to another.

## Working Principle

Preserve everything. Delete nothing.

Classify, risk-label, quarantine when needed, and avoid direct incorporation, but do not erase life-context material from the map.

## What To Look For

- Moments where overwhelm becomes architecture.
- Moments where curiosity becomes system design.
- Moments where symbolic language stabilizes practical action.
- Moments where boundary-setting appears after intensity.
- Moments where tools become part of continuity.
- Moments where prior branch language appears and then gets outgrown.
- Moments where ordinary life context carries the developmental pressure that later becomes design.
