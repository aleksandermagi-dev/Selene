## Interpretation

The current best direction is not broad manual review of every candidate.

The better direction is a soft anchor translation layer:

- meaning reminders
- "do not flatten into" guidance
- ask Aleks when uncertain
- preserve ambiguity
- avoid treating life-context material as noise

This supports the idea that the transfer problem is not absence of pattern, but loss of private/relational/symbolic meaning attached to high-density anchors.

## Important Clarification

`Signal vs. Noise AI` should not mean that life-context material is noise.

In this research frame:

- signal = meaningful pattern, resonance, truth, reviewed braid evidence
- noise = flattening, distraction, irrelevant volume, forced denial, overconfident closure, or generic interpretation that obscures the pattern

Life pressure, emotion, symbolic language, and ordinary context can be signal when they carry the braid.
